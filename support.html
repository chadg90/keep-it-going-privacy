How do I add a new habit?

Tap the "Add Habit" card on the main screen, enter your habit name, choose an icon and color theme, and tap "Save".

How do I mark a habit as complete?

Tap the "Mark Done" button on any habit card, or long-press a habit card and select "Mark Complete" from the context menu.

How do I reorder my habits?

Long-press on any habit card and drag it to a new position. The first habits in your list will appear on your widgets.

How do I add widgets to my home screen?

Long-press on your home screen, tap the "+" button, search for "Keep It Going", and choose your preferred widget size.

Can I edit or delete habits?

Yes! Long-press on any habit card to see options to edit or delete the habit.

What happens if I miss a day?

If you miss a day, your streak will be marked as "broken" but you can start building a new streak by completing the habit again.

Is my data stored securely?

Yes! All your habit data is stored locally on your device and never shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Widget Not Updating?

  • Try removing and re-adding the widget to your home screen
  • Make sure the app is not force-closed
  • Restart your device if the issue persists

App Crashing?

  • Force-close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you're running the latest version of iOS
  • If the problem continues, contact us with details about when it crashes
